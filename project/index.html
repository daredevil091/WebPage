Diet Food

Meat

Meat is animal tissue used as food. Most often is used to describe skeletal muscle and fat that is found with it. Types of meat include beef and veal from cattle, pork, ham and bacon from pigs, mutton from sheep, venison from deer, fish, insects, and poultry from chickens, ducks and turkeys

Sea Food

Seafood is shellfish such as lobsters, mussels, and crabs, and sometimes other sea creatures that you can eat.

Hot & Spicy

Spicy can mean full of spices (flavors), including cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, garlic…as well as containing things like piperine and capsaicin that give your mouth a burning sensation like from pepper and chili. Hot can mean physically hot that will literally burn your mouth as well as percieved heat from chemicals.
